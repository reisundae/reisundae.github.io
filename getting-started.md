The War Room
The adventures you go on depend on what hooks you book, but there are several ways hooks can get generated: 
World events. Most of the time it's generated by the GMs. Events in-world that need to be resolved become the hooks you normally see. 
Results. Sometimes the actions of the players in one mission can have such a powerful effect that it creates problems or opportunities for another mission. In this case the hook says: Result.
Player generated. The most valuable hooks though, are those the players create. Players can submit objectives to the GMs to be made into hooks that they can pursue. These objectives can be either from their backstory (ex. I want to find my sister) or from their ambitions (ex. I want to build a fortress in the forest)..
